# Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Amazon e-commerce sales data using SQL and Power BI to uncover revenue trends, operational inefficiencies, fulfilment performance, and business growth opportunities.

The analysis focuses on sales performance, cancellation rates, product categories, regional demand, and fulfilment effectiveness to generate actionable business insights.

---

## Dataset Information

* Total Records Analyzed: 110,878
* Dataset: Amazon Sales Data
* Domain: E-Commerce
* Tools Used:

  * MySQL
  * Power BI
  * SQL

---

## Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project were:

* Analyze overall business performance.
* Measure total revenue and average order value.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Evaluate cancellation trends and operational risks.
* Compare Amazon and Merchant fulfilment performance.
* Discover high-performing and underperforming regions.
* Provide actionable business recommendations.

## Key Business Insights

### 1. Strong Revenue Generation

The business generated approximately ₹67.39 million in revenue, demonstrating strong customer demand and significant market presence within the e-commerce apparel segment.

---

### 2. High Cancellation Rate

The overall cancellation rate is approximately 14.5%, indicating opportunities to improve fulfilment efficiency, inventory planning, and customer experience.

---

### 3. Set Category Drives Revenue

The Set category generated the highest revenue at approximately ₹34.15 million, making it the most important product category for the business.

---

### 4. Heavy Dependence on Set and Kurta Categories

Set and Kurta categories together contributed more than ₹52 million in revenue, indicating that a substantial portion of the business relies on these two categories.

---

### 5. Revenue-Generating Categories Also Experience High Cancellations

Set (14.58%) and Kurta (14.62%) recorded some of the highest cancellation rates among major categories, directly impacting revenue retention.

---

### 6. Maharashtra Is the Strongest Market

Maharashtra generated the highest revenue (₹11.44 million) and recorded the highest order volume, making it the most valuable market for the business.

---

### 7. Karnataka Is a Key Growth Market

Karnataka generated ₹8.86 million in revenue and emerged as the second-largest revenue-generating state.

---

### 8. Operational Challenges in Uttar Pradesh and Telangana

Uttar Pradesh (15.03%) and Telangana (14.52%) reported higher cancellation rates than Maharashtra and Karnataka, indicating operational inefficiencies requiring further investigation.

---

### 9. Amazon Fulfilment Outperforms Merchant Fulfilment

Amazon fulfilment generated ₹45.97 million in revenue across 71,269 orders while maintaining a lower cancellation rate (12.77%) than Merchant fulfilment (17.45%).

---

### 10. Significant Opportunity for Revenue Retention

Reducing cancellations in high-revenue categories and improving merchant fulfilment operations could significantly increase retained revenue and customer satisfaction.

## Business Recommendations

### 1. Reduce Cancellations in High-Revenue Categories

Since Set and Kurta generate the majority of revenue, reducing cancellations in these categories should be a top priority.

### 2. Improve Merchant Fulfilment Operations

Merchant fulfilment experiences significantly higher cancellation rates and requires process optimization.

### 3. Focus on High-Performing Markets

Continue investing in Maharashtra and Karnataka due to their strong revenue contribution and operational stability.

### 4. Investigate High-Cancellation Regions

Conduct detailed operational reviews in Uttar Pradesh and Telangana to identify the causes of elevated cancellation rates.

### 5. Improve Inventory Forecasting

Implement data-driven inventory planning to reduce stock-related cancellations and improve order fulfilment efficiency.
