-- # Top 10 Business Insights & Recommendations

-- ## 1. Total Revenue Performance

-- The business generated a total revenue of ₹67.39 million during the analysis period, demonstrating strong customer demand and significant market presence in the e-commerce apparel segment.

-- ---

-- ## 2. High Cancellation Rate Identified

-- The overall cancellation rate was approximately 14.5%, indicating a major operational challenge that may be impacting customer satisfaction and revenue retention.

-- ---

-- ## 3. Set Category Dominates Revenue

-- The Set category generated the highest revenue (₹34.15 million), making it the most important product category for the business.

-- ---

-- ## 4. Heavy Dependence on Set and Kurta Categories

-- Set and Kurta categories together generated over ₹52 million in revenue, indicating that a large portion of the business depends on these two product categories.

-- ---

-- ## 5. High-Revenue Categories Also Have High Cancellation Rates

-- Set (14.58%) and Kurta (14.62%) recorded the highest cancellation rates among major categories. Since these categories drive most revenue, reducing cancellations here would significantly improve business performance.

-- ---

-- ## 6. Maharashtra Is the Strongest Market

-- Maharashtra generated the highest revenue (₹11.44 million) and the highest order volume (19,142 orders), making it the company's most valuable market.

-- ---

-- ## 7. Karnataka Emerges as a Key Growth Market

-- Karnataka generated ₹8.86 million in revenue with 14,711 orders, establishing itself as the second most important market for the business.

-- ---

-- ## 8. Uttar Pradesh and Telangana Require Operational Attention

-- Although Uttar Pradesh and Telangana contribute substantial revenue, they exhibit higher cancellation rates (15.03% and 14.52%), suggesting operational inefficiencies that require investigation.

-- ---

-- ## 9. Amazon Fulfilment Outperforms Merchant Fulfilment

-- Amazon fulfilment generated ₹45.97 million in revenue across 71,269 orders while maintaining a lower cancellation rate (12.77%) than Merchant fulfilment (17.45%). This demonstrates superior operational efficiency.

-- ---

-- ## 10. Strategic Recommendation

-- To improve profitability and customer experience, the business should prioritize Amazon fulfilment, reduce cancellations in Set and Kurta categories, strengthen operations in Uttar Pradesh and Telangana, and continue investing in high-performing markets such as Maharashtra and Karnataka.
